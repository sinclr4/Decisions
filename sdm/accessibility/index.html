ACCESSIBILITY

Accessibilty Statement

This section explains how we ensure that the NHS Shared Decision Making (SDM) Website is usable and accessible to all users. The website follows the Central Office of Information guidelines for UK government websites and is fully compliant with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0, level AA.

This accessibility statement applies only to the NHS SDM Website. It does not cover:

  • other websites operated by or for the NHS or the Department of Health
  • other government and agency websites
  • any other site that we link to from this website

Access Keys And Shortcuts For NHS SDM Website

Access keys are keyboard shortcuts that allow you to go directly to specific parts of this site.  They are very useful for anyone who does not use a pointing device, such as a mouse.  The NHS SDM Website follows the UK government suggested access keys standard for websites, except for cases where these keys interfer with browser specific shortcut keys.  The NHS SDM Website access keys are:

  • Alt + N (skip to main navigation)
  • Alt + S (skip navigation)
  • Alt + shift + 0 (access keys information)
  • Alt + shift + 1 (homepage)
  • Alt + shift + 8 (terms and conditions)
  • Alt + shift + 9 (contact us)
  • Feedback form
  • Privacy Policy
