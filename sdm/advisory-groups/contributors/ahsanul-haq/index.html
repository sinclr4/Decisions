AHSANUL HAQ

Consultant Urological Surgeon

Mr. Ahsanul Haq is a Consultant Urological Surgeon at Lancashire Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust. He has a specialist interest the management of BPH with laser therapy. Mr. Haq is actively involved in Urological teaching at an undergarduate and graduate level. He is also a faculty member of a number of Urological Courses at the Royal College of Surgeons London and Edinburgh and has published a number of articles relating to BPH and its management.
