AIDAN FOWLER

Colorectal Surgeon

Aidan Fowler has been a Consultant Colorectal Surgeon in Gloucestershire for 10 years following qualifying at UCL in 1990 and then training in London and the South West. He is a member of the ACPGBI Multidisciplinary committee and is a faculty member of the South West Patient Safety Programme who regularly teaches on Healthcare Improvement, Patient Safety and Non Technical Skills in Safety as well as Colorectal Disease.
