AILSA BOSWORTH

CEO National Rheumatoid Arthritis Society

With a background in business management and marketing in the fields of engineering, computing and audio visual services, Ailsa Bosworth is the Founder and Chief Executive of the National Rheumatoid Arthritis Society. NRAS are acknowledged as the voice for people with RA in the UK and provide a wide range of services for people affected by RA including self management training (in partnership with the Expert Patients Programme). NRAS has grown substantially in a short space of time and now employs 21 staff.

Ailsa has had sero-negative RA for over 30 years and contributes at a national level to committees steering groups and working parties such asNICETechnology Appraisals, British Society for Rheumatology Guideline Development Groups and the Biologic Registers, and was on the Guideline Development Group for the NICE RA Guidelines published in 2009. She is currently working as part of the NICE Topic Expert Group producing a Quality Standard in RA.

She was on the Expert Panel advising the National Audit Office in respect of their landmark report published in July 2009. She was Joint Chair of the Rheumatology Futures Project Group which was responsible for commissioning the King’s Fund Report into RA and the subsequent inflammatory arthritis commissioning pathway. NRAS supports a huge amount of academic research in the UK.
