ANDREW BRADLEY

Professor of Surgery, University of Cambridge

Andrew Bradley is Professor of Surgery and Head of the University Department of Surgery at the University of Cambridge. He is an honorary Consultant Transplant Surgeon at Addenbrooke's Hospital Cambridge and was Clinical Director of Transplantation at Addenbrooke's Hospital from 2001 to 2011. Andrew is a past President of the British Transplantation Society and is a Fellow of the Academy of Medical sciences. He is currently Chairman of the Kidney Advisory Group at NHS Blood and Transplant.

Andrew's clinical interest is kidney transplantation and his research interests are in transplantation immunology and aspects of clinical organ transplantation. He has published numerous scientific papers and is Editor of the journal Transplantation and Associate Editor of the American Journal of Transplantation.
