ANNE STEVENSON

National Programme Manager, NHS AAA Screening Programme

Anne Stevenson is the National Programme Manager of the NHS Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm Screening Programme (NAAASP) and the NHS Diabetic Eye Screening Programme (NDESP) and has worked in the NHS for over 25 years with experience of service delivery and management. She trained as an Audiologist and worked in this field for 17 years until 2002 when she took her first national screening programme role, working in the NHS Newborn Hearing Screening Programme. In January 2008 she began the setting up of the NHS AAA Screening Programme ready for the start of implementation in 2009. She is currently National Programme Manager of the NHS Adult (non cancer) Screening Programmes, which includes the AAA Programme and the DES Programme, both of which are based in Gloucester.
