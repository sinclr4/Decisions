AVRIL SURRIDGE

Chair, St Peter's and District Branch Diabetes UK

Avril Surridge is a retired Primary Headteacher with three children and four grandchildren. She has had Type 1 diabetes for over 37 years and witnessed many improved changes in its treatment!

Since retirement she has become increasingly involved with the NHS and Diabetes UK and has worked with such groups as NHS Diabetes, the Classification of Diabetes Group and the Year of Care Programme. Locally, she is Chair of the Diabetes UK Voluntary Group, a member of the local hospital Patient Panel and several internal hospital groups.

She has worked with a number of Long Term Conditions groups in the South East and was a member of Diabetes UK Advisory Council for three years. She has made a number of presentations both locally and nationally for, among others, Diabetes UK, the Patient Information Forum, NHS Surrey, the South East Coast Strategic Health Authority, PCTs beyond the region, South East Coast Ambulance Trust and many patient groups. She has established wide networks and is a member of LinkedIn.

Currently her main enthusiasms are the provision of Structured Patient Education, accredited information for all people with Long Term Conditions, patients being equal partners in their care planning and health and social care management and the equality of care for all.
