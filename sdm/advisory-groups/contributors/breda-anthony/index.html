BREDA ANTHONY

Nurse Consultant Colposcopy/ Chair UK Nurse Colposcopy Group

Breda Anthony R.N,R.M, BSc, MSc, PHD student is currently working at Edinburgh Royal Infirmary as well as Aberdeen University as a Research Fellow. She trained at Old church Hospital in Romford Essex and midwifery at Whipps Cross Hospital, East London During her colposcopy specialist training at Aberdeen Royal Infirmary she worked on a RCT study to look at pain experienced by women during outpatient treatment at colposcopy. This subsequently led to her own research for her PHD looking at ways to improve care and treatment for women attending colposcopy clinics. During this time she became involved with the RCN Colposcopy Nurses Group and took on the role of newsletter editor for the group. In 2006 she took over the role of chair of this group who are now affiliated with the BSCCP and currently in this role. She is the colposcopy nurse advisor for the RCN Women’s Health Forum’ She currently working as a Nurse consultant two days a week at the Royal in Edinburgh and is involved in research and teaching there as well as at Aberdeen University. She is a reviewer for the Journal of Public Health. Special interest is in all aspects of women’s health and wellbeing as well as supporting colposcopy trainee nurses and organising a colposcopy nurse’s conference on an annual basis.
