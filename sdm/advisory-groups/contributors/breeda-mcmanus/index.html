BREEDA MC MANUS

Renal Consultant Nurse

I currently hold the post of Renal Consultant Nurse at Barts Health. I have worked in Renal Medicine for the past 15 years holding a number of varying positions within the kidney field.

My current role involves me managing “late presenters” to service and a cohort of our haemodialysis programme. I have a strong interest in ways of improving the experience of kidney patients and am passionate about ensuring we empower and engage our kidney patients throughout their pathway.

I am involved in a number of service development projects with the aim to ensure our kidney patients encounter the best experience achievable. I am also an active member of the Renal Advisory Board for the Department of Health.
