CATHERINE BLACKABY

National Improvement Lead – Lung Improvement Programme. NHS Improvement

Catherine is currently National Improvement Lead with the Lung team at

NHS Improvement where she leads work on managing COPD as a long term
condition. Catherine has over 20 years experience of working in the NHS,
including service development and redesign work in primary, community
and urgent care and as an operational manager of hospital and community
services. Catherine was a Fellow of the NHS Institute for Innovation &
Improvement in 2007 / 2008, she is a member of the Improvement Faculty
for Quality & Patient Safety, and has a particular interest in the human and
cultural aspects of change.
