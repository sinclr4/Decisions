CATHY BALL

Research Clinical Specialist Hand Therapist

Cathy Ball works as research clinical specialist hand therapist in the Kennedy Institute,UniversityofOxford. She is an Occupational Therapist with a particular interest in outcome measures used in hand therapy and investigates how people’s hand function is affected by different conditions, aiming to accurately reflect the patient’s experience. She previously worked as a specialist clinician in the NHS and obtained a Masters degree in Health Sciences fromUniversityofEast Anglia. Cathy is also on the editorial committee for Hand Therapy journal.
