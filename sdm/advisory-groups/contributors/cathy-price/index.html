CATHY PRICE

NIHR advisor/Pain Consultant

Dr Cathy Price combines the roles of consultant in pain medicine at the in University Hospital of Southampton FT with a focus on highly complex cases, a consultant advisor to the NIHR Evaluation, Trials and Studies Coordinating Centre and clinical director of Southampton City Clinical Commissioning Group.

In her commissioning role she has been vice chair of a professional executive committee - one of two consultants employed to support the local primary care trust in commissioning. During this time she oversaw implementation of a major clinical pathways project in Southampton. Her current portfolio includes Musculoskeletal and Diabetes services.

She is currently clinical lead to the National Pain Audit on specialist pain services and has recently overseen the British Pain Society’s persistent pain pathways project as one of the editorial board.

In her hospital role she is developing inpatient services for complex pain patients and care planning for frequent attenders in conjunction with A & E.
