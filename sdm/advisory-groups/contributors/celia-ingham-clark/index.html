CELIA INGHAM CLARK

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Associate Medical Director, Whittington Health and Associate Medical Director, NHS London

Celia Ingham Clark trained in colorectal surgery in London and was appointed as a consultant at the Whittington Hospital in 1996. She was National Clinical Lead for Colorectal Cancer with the Cancer Services Collaborative and then the Modernisation Agency between 2004-2007. From 2007-2013 she has been National Clinical Lead for Transforming Inpatient Care for people with cancer, working with NHS Improvement. She is a member of the Clinical Governance Board of the Association of Coloproctology of Great Britain and Ireland.

She was Medical Director of Whittington Hospital Trust from 2004 to 2012 and is now Associate Medical Director for revalidation and quality at NHS London.

Celia has contributed to producing information for patients with colorectal cancer through a video for Whittington Hospital patients and for the NHS Choices website, and contributed to colorectal pathways in Map of Medicine. She is interested in raising quality by improving pathways of patient care.
