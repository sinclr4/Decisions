CHARLES CAMPION-SMITH

GP & Macmillan GP Advisor

Dr Charles Campion-Smith was a General Practitioner in Dorchester, Dorset for
over 30 years.

For most of this time he has been active in primary care and interprofessional
education. He is a Macmillan GP Advisor, a visiting fellow at the School of Health
and Social Care, Bournemouth University, and previously a Board member of the
UK Centre for the Advancement of Interprofessional Education (CAIPE) .

His research interests include interprofessional learning, communication, cancer
and end-of-life care.

He has published papers on general practitioner education, interprofessional
learning and continuous quality improvement in primary care.

Other interests include his expanding family and recent grandparenthood,
paragliding and exploring the countryside and coast in Dorset and beyond on foot,
bicycle and by sailing dinghy.
