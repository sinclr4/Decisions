CHRIS DEIGHTON

MB BS(Hons) BMedSci(Hons) MD FRCP, President of the British Society for Rheumatology, Consultant Rheumatologist, Royal Derby Hospital, Honorary Associate Professor, School of Medical & Surgical Sciences, Nottingham University

I qualified in 1984 atNewcastleMedicalSchool, and undertook my rheumatology training in the North-East. My MD thesis was on the genetic epidemiology of rheumatoid arthritis. In 1993 I was awarded a MRC Travelling Fellowship toBerkeleyUniversityto extend my expertise in genetic analysis. In 1993 I also started my first Consultant job inNottingham, and was Clinical Director in Rheumatology for 5 years. I was attracted to a new consultant job inDerbyfollowing the opening of theGraduateEntryMedicalSchoolin 2003. My NHS post has protected time for research and delivering and developing undergraduate teaching.

Current positions include:

President of the British Society for RheumatologyClinical Advisor to the NICE Rheumatoid Arthritis Management Guidelines.Chair of the Specialised Rheumatology Clinical Reference GroupLead on the British Society for Rheumatology Biologics GuidelinesLead for the Musculoskeletal Specialist Interest Group,Trent Clinical Research Network
