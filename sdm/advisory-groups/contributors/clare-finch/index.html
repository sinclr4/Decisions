CLARE FINCH

Research Nurse - Dermatology

I qualified as a Registered Nurse ( Adult branch ) from Leeds College of Health in 1996. After spells working in Day Surgery, Maxillo-Facial, General & Ophthalmic Theatres, I joined the Dermatology Department at Harrogate District Hospital in August 2004. I divide my time between working as a staff nurse in Dermatology & as a research nurse for acne & psoriasis studies. I help recruit for an acne genetics study & also treat patients , who require hyfrecation for macrocomedomes.

For the last 7 years I have been involved in a study " improved baseline assessments in acne " , whereby I see new referrals in order to aid treatment outcomes & to compile a database to help find out more about the causes & consequences of acne & how best to treat it. We currently have over 1200 patients on the database.
