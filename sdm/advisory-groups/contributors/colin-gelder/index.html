COLIN GELDER

Respiratory Physician

Colin Gelder qualified form University College Hospital (UCH) in 1984, and trained at UCH, The Whittington Hospital, John Radcliffe and Churchill Hospitals Oxford, The Brompton Hospital/National Heart and Lung Institute (NHLI), and The National Hospital Queen Square. He undertook research leading to a PhD at the NHLI and Imperial College London . He was a Senior Lecturer/Consultant in Respiratory Medicine at Llandough Hospital Cardiff for about 12 years and relocated, to University Hospital Coventry in March 2009. He has a special interest in airway diseases and is one of the two Respiratory leads for the West Midlands SHA. Dr Gelder is the clinical lead for the new Integrated COPD Service in Coventry and is a member of the British Thoracic Society's (BTS) COPD and Data Special Advisory Groups and the BTS Standards of Care Committee.
