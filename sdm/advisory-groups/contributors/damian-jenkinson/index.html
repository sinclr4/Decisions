About Dr Damian Jenkinson:

Dr Damian Jenkinson has been National Clinical Lead for the NHS Stroke Improvement Programme since 2007, was formerly Associate Director of the UK Stroke Research Network for Service Development and Training, and chaired the Workforce Group of the Department of Health’s National Stroke Strategy.

Dr Jenkinson has contributed to wider NHS policy and practice as Associate Medical Director to the Chief Medical Officer’s Group, and as Medical Director of the NHS Clinical Governance Support Team.

Dr Jenkinson continues clinical practice as Consultant Stroke Physician at The Royal Bournemouth and Christchurch Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust.
