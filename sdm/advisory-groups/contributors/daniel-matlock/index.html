DANIEL MATLOCK

Assistant Professor of Medicine

Dan Matlock, MD is an Assistant Professor in the Division of General Internal Medicine at the University of Colorado Denver. Clinically, he is a geriatrician working in outpatient general medicine and inpatient palliative care. His research interest is on decision making surrounding aggressive interventions, particularly among older and chronically ill populations. Currently he is focusing on developing interventions to improve the decision making surrounding implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICD).
