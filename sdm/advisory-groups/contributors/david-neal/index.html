DAVID NEAL

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Professor of Surgical Oncology

Professor Neal was appointed in 2002 to the Foundation Chair of Surgical Oncology in Cambridge. Prior to that, he held the Chairmanship of the School of Surgical and Reproductive Sciences in the University of Newcastle upon Tyne, where he was also Director of Research. He has developed clinical programmes in major urological cancer surgery, including robotic surgery and has published over 500 peer-reviewed articles and books. He has always had a strong interest in clinical audit and patient- reported outcomes and believes strongly that patients should take a leading part in decision-making as a full partner with advice from their medical team. He has been a Council Member on the King’s Fund, Council Member of the Royal College of Surgeons of England, Council Member of the Academy of Medical Sciences and also has been appointed to work on the previous RAE committees for HEFCE. He is a senior faculty member of the National Institute of Health Research in the UK.
