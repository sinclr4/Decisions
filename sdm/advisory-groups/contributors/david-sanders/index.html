DAVID SANDERS

Surgeon Registrar

David Sanders is a surgical registrar in the South West Peninsula. He graduated from Edinburgh University Medical School and is a member of the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh. He has been awarded several prizes and distinctions throughout his training and developed an interest in hernia surgery after being involved with the charity ‘Operation Hernia’ in Ghana and Ecuador. He has recently completed his MD thesis on hernia mesh morphology and reducing infection risk after abdominal wall hernia repair. He is co-chair of the NHS commissioning guidance for groin hernia surgery and is widely published in the field of hernia surgery.
