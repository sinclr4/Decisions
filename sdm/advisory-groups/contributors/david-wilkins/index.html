DAVID WILKINS

Policy Officer, Men’s Health Forum

David has written policy papers on several aspects of male health, including men’smental health; men’s sexual health; male obesity; and cancer in men. He has alsowritten a number of national-level reports, most recently, reviews of evidence andpractice in male mental health. He has managed several large scale projects aimedat improving male health; between 2007 and 2009 for example, he led a three yearresearch project aiming to increase men’s participation in the NHS Bowel Cancer Screening Programme. David represents the “men’s health interest” on a number ofnational and regional policy development bodies.
