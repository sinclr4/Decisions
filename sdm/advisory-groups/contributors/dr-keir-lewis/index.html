KEIR LEWIS

Reader in Medicine and Honorary Chest Consultant

Keir Lewis is a Reader in Medicine in Swansea University and Honorary Chest Physician in Hywel Dda Health Board, South Wales. He is R&D Director for Hywel Dda and a member of the National Institute for Social Care and Health Research (NISCHR ) implementation group. .After training and junior posts in London, he completed his specialist training and MD thesis in South Wales.

He has published various papers, book chapters and diplomas on smoking cessation, is editor of the textbook ‘A Clinicians Guide to Smoking Cessation’ and associate editor for smoking cessation for ‘International Journal Tubercle and Lung Disease.’ He reviews on tobacco matters for NICE, HTA and advises the Welsh Assembly on their 2012 Tobacco Action Plan.

He is a member of the European Respiratory Society Task Force on Tobacco Control chairs and the British Thoracic Society (BTS) Tobacco Specialist Advisory Group. He co-authored the current BTS recommendations for Smoking Cessation in Secondary Care.
