ELIZABETH ENGLAND

NIHR Senior Clinical Lecturer

Liz England is an NIHR Senior Clinical Lecturer in Primary Care. Liz has published several papers in scientific journals and contributed to chapters in books in the field of primary care mental health. She has received a number of grants from the NIHR. Liz’s research interests are focused on the areas of primary care mental health policy, the interface and collaborative working between primary and secondary care mental health services and early intervention for first episode psychosis. Liz is also a GP a busy inner city practice in Birmingham where she leads on mental health and delivers Graduate Entry Course medical student teaching.
Liz has a number of additional roles including Appraisal Lead for the Birmingham and Solihull NHS cluster, Primary Care Lead for the Heart of England Hub of the MHRN, Mental Health representative for the BBC CLRN and Mental Health and Learning Disability Lead for Healthworks LCG (Sandwell and West Birmingham CCG).
