EMMA MALCOLM

Former CEO Prostate Action

Emma joined the Prostate Cancer Research Foundation in 2005 as the first permanent member of staff. In 2010 the Prostate Cancer Research Foundation merged with Prostate UK and Emma was appointed Chief Executive of Prostate Action.  When Prostate Action merged with Prostate Cancer UK in Sept 2012 Emma decided it was time to leave the prostate world.

Emma sat on the steering committee for UK Prostate Link, the Prostate Cancer Charter for Action, the James Lind Alliance and the Prostate Cancer Advisory Group. She was also a trustee of the Prostate Cancer Support Federation.

Emma has a background in fundraising, and was responsible for the day to day running of Prostate Action. She ran the 2012 London Marathon for Prostate Action raising over £5,600, and was a musician in a former life.
