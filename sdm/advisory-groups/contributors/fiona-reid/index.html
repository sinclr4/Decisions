FIONA REID

Nurse Consultant

Fiona has worked in Dermatology for 22 years in a variety of roles. She is employed by Galderma UK a company dedicated to dermatology education. Fiona has worked for Galderma since 2007 and delivers education on acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea skin cancer and PDT. She also performs a service development role where she helps set up primary care dermatology clinics and trains the nursing teams.

Fiona maintains an NHS honorary contract in both Leeds and Harrogate. In Harrogate she runs an acne clinic treating all forms of acne, initiating and monitoring patients on isotretinoin. In Leeds she runs a systemic monitoring service for chronic skin conditions and has extensive experience in Biologic therapies, where she works closely with the rheumatologists.

Fiona currently both chairs the Northern & Yorkshire Biologics Group and is the secretary of the British Dermatology Nursing Group.
