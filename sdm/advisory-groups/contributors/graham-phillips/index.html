GRAHAM PHILLIPS

Pharmacist

I have a special interest in Pharmacy and Public Health, Men’s health in particular. I am involved in the steering group on a DH-sponsored project looking at increasing Men’s use of Community Pharmacy (due to be published later this year)

I had significant input into the DH “Public Health Through Pharmacy” document in which my model pharmacy in Letchworth, Herts, is heavily featured (DH, 2006)

Involved in DH Lung Cancer Awareness Month Working Group. The work of this group was one of the exemplars in DH White Paper, Pharmacy in England (2008)

Our group of pharmacies works closely with the School of Pharmacy, University of Hatfield. Currently working to establish a model for a “Teaching Pharmacy” which we believe will be crucial to implementation of “Pharmacy in England”

I am one of a the research team, working with Herts PCT, the University f Hertfordshire School of Pharmacy and the post-graduate medical school in a trial to establish the value of intensive interventions by community pharmacists in patients with “metabolic syndrome”. This project, due to complete in late 2010, was runner up, PJ Care Awards, 2009.
