HERMIONE PRICE

Consultant Nephrologist

Hermione Price is a locum consultant in Diabetes and Endocrinology in Oxford and currently spends the majority of her time seconded to the Diabetes Policy Team at the Department of Health and to NHS Diabetes where she is working on the CVD workstream. Her clinical and research interests include the management of CVD risk in people with type 2 diabetes. She holds a DPhil is behaviour change modification in individuals at increased CVD risk and has authored numerous peer-reviewed publications in this area.
