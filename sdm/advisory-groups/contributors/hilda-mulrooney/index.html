HILDA MULROONEY

Dietitian & nutrition lecturer, representing domUk, a specialist group of the BDA

Hilda Mulrooney is a Registered Dietitian with a PhD in Nutrition. She is currently working as a Senior Lecturer in Nutrition at Kingston University in London, having previously worked in public health, primary care dietetics and other academic posts. She is an active committee member of Dietitians in Obesity Management UK (domUK), a specialist group of the British Dietetic Association, and was Training Officer for domUk for four years. She regularly facilitates on behaviour change training courses for healthcare professionals and develops responses from domUK  to other organisations including NICE.
