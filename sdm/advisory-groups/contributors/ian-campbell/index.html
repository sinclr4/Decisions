IAN CAMPBELL

Medical Advisory Group Lead

Dr Ian Campbell is a Nottingham GP who specialises in obesity and weight loss management. He was awarded an MBE in the Queen's Birthday Honours in 2009 for his services to medicine. In 2000 Ian founded the national Obesity Forum and served as Chair till 2005, following which he was the Honorary Medical Director of the UCL charity Weight Concern until 2012. A frequent contributor to national TV, radio, and the press, Ian co-authored his book 'Obesity: Your Questions Answered' in 2005. He launched www.fatmanslim.com, a men only weight loss website in 2004. Other roles include Chair of the NHS East Midlands Carbon Reduction Steering Project.
