IAN MCPHERSON

Clinical Psychologist, Director of Improving Health and Wellbeing UK

A Clinical Psychologist by professional background, Ian worked as a clinician and trainer, before moving into senior managerial positions in the NHS and then onto policy implementation in mental health.

As Director of the National Mental Health Development Unit and of the National Institute for Mental Health in England he was closely involved in overseeing the implementation of the first wave of the Improving Access to Psychological Therapies (IAPT) Programme.

He now works as a Director, Advisor and Non–Executive Director with a number of organisations in the NHS, voluntary and independent sectors, including the Mental Health Providers Forum, which is the representative body for voluntary sector mental health providers.

He was awarded the OBE for services to mental health in 2011.
