JESME FOX

Medical Director, Roy Castle Lung Cancer Foundation

Joined Roy Castle Lung Cancer Foundation in November 1998, and currently holds the post of Medical Director. She regularly provides comment to a variety of TV, radio and print media. She has written many articles, raising public awareness of this disease and focusing on lung cancer patient issues. She leads the Foundation’s Policy work and work associated with its Lung Cancer Research Grants portfolio. Amongst her committee work, she is an elected steering group member of the Cancer Campaigning Group, Secretary of the Global Lung Cancer Coalition of advocacy organisations and is a member of the International Association for the Study of Lung Cancer’s Education and Advocacy Committees.
