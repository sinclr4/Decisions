JO LARKIN

Sport & Exercise Medicine SpR

Dr Jo Larkin is a Sport and Exercise Medicine Speciality Trainee in the London Deanery. She has been based at UCLH for 12 months and was involved in developing an exercise programme for the medical bariatric patients at UCLH. During her training she has also been involved in several public health projects acting as an advisory role for the benefits of exercise for health. She is also a contributory author to the Department of Health document SEM: A fresh approach.
