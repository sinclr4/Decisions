JOANNE RILEY

Respiratory Nurse Specialist

Jo Riley RGN, INP (Independent nurse prescriber)

Current role – Lead for Respiratory and Home Oxygen Service, Chair ARNS (Association of respiratory Nurse Specialists)

I am currently working as the lead for Respiratory and Home oxygen Services in Oxfordshire, having set up a fully integrated service across hospital and community, providing holistic care for patients with COPD; including pulmonary rehabilitation and oxygen assessment and review in the hospital and the community.

My main areas of interest are excellence in care for COPD patients, in particular standards of spirometry. I addition I have also supported many areas setting up oxygen assessment and review services following the success of our service locally.

I am a trainer for Education for Health and my most recent work with them has been working in Bangladesh in a project to improve asthma care through high quality education and training. I regularly present at national meetings and also run local training courses.

I have been an ARNS member since it was established and sat on the original committee setting up the association during its first 4 years. I rejoined the committee 4 years ago and am now Chair of the association. I am also a member of the BTS, ERS and PCRS.
