JOHN COPPING

Director, Carers in Partnership CIC

A qualified leader of NHS service improvement, John works alongside commissioners and providers to help them engage carers towards service improvement, involving also third sector provider bodies. His working life majored in productivity, performance and quality improvement, change management, and innovation through partnership working. A member of Warwickshire MH Compact and a LINk board currently in transition to HealthWatch, John directs operations for the regional social enterprise Carers in Partnership CIC.
