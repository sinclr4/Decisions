JOHN WHITE

Chair of the National Lung Cancer Forum for Nurses

John White is the Lead Macmillan Lung Cancer Nurse Specialist based at Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust and is the current Chair of the National Lung Cancer Forum for Nurses (NLCFN).

His representation of the NLCFN on key UK national groups include the Department of Health Lung Cancer and Mesothelioma Advisory Group, the British Thoracic Society Lung Cancer and Mesothelioma Specialist Advisory Group and the United Kingdom Lung Cancer Coalition.

He also represents the NLCFN on the Global Lung Cancer Coalition, is a Committee member of the International Thoracic Oncology Nursing Forum and the International Association for the Study of Lung Cancer AHP group.
