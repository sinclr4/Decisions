JON REES

General Practitioner

Dr Jon Rees is a GP partner in Nailsea, North Somerset with an interest in Men's Health and Urology. He was on the Guideline Development Group for the 2010 NICE Lower Urinary Tract Symptoms in Men clinical guideline and is on the editorial board of Trends in Urology and Men's Health. He helps to run a community Urology service in Bristol, North Somerset and South Gloucestershire.
