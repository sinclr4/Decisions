JONOTHAN EARNSHAW

Medical Advisory Group Lead: National Programme Director, NHS AAA Screening Programme

Jonothan qualified from Guy’s Hospital, London, in 1979 and is a Consultant Vascular Surgeon at Gloucestershire Royal Hospital. As National Programme Director, he has overseen the implementation of the NHS Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm Screening Programme in England, working closely with the UK National Screening Committee and the Department of Health. Jonothan was Honorary Secretary of the Vascular Society of Great Britain and Ireland (VSGBI) from 2006 to 2010, previous Chair of the VSGBI’s Audit Committee, a member of the Society’s Professional Standards and Audit Committees and Chair elect of the Professional Standards Committee.
