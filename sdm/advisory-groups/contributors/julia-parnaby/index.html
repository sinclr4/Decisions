JULIA PARNABY

Head of Knowledge and Information

Julia Parnaby is Head of Knowledge and Information at the Stroke Association where her team runs the national stroke helpline and produces the Stroke Association’s suite of patient information materials. With over 20 years experience in the information field she previously worked as a librarian in the academic and public sectors before moving into the voluntary/advice sector in 2000. She is a trustee at an umbrella organisation for the helpline sector and a reviewer for the BMA's Patient Information Awards. Julia has a degree in Politics and Sociology and post graduate qualifications in Information Studies and Translation (French to English).
