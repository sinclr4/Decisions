JUNAID BAJWA

GP

Dr Junaid Bajwa is a GP in Greenwich, and one of the newest members to the CCG Board, and is currently working towards completing his Masters in Healthcare Commissioning at the University of Birmingham. He has a keen interest in education and his current portfolio includes being a GP Appraiser for NHS South East London, a Programme Director for Greenwich's Vocational GP Training Scheme, and a Director of Essential Guides (an organisation delivering national courses for medical professionals).
