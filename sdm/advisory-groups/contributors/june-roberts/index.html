JUNE ROBERTS

Consultant Nurse

June leads integrated respiratory services across primary and secondary care with strong emphasis on joint working with general practice. The Salford integrated model has demonstrated improved outcomes for patients and the local health economy. June is also joint respiratory clinical pathway lead for NHS North west, sits on PCRS-UK executive and is a regional committee member of the British Lung Foundation. June has many years experience of working in general practice and in research and has published widely.
