JUSTIN CLARK

Consultant Obstetrician & Gynaecologist

Justin Clarkis a Consultant in Obstetrics and Gynaecology at the Birmingham Women’s Hospital and Honorary Reader at theUniversityofBirmingham. His clinical and research interests lie in the management of general gynaecological conditions, especially the diagnosis and treatment of menstrual disorders, fibroids, pelvic pain and endometriosis. He is Chair of the RCOG Clinical Studies Group in Menstrual Disorders, Endometriosis & Gynaecological Endoscopy. He holds several substantial research grants in women’s health including studies into the diagnosis and treatment of menstrual disorders. Since 2009 he has been a scientific editor on the British Journal of Obstetrics & Gynaecology. He lectures nationally and internationally and has published over 70 research papers, two books and guidelines for evidence-based gynaecological practice.
