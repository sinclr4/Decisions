KAREN ANNE HURSEY

Patient Representative

Karen has been in the Hairdressing profession for 30 years and is now a Style Director. In 2011 she suffered a sudden onset of severe acne (Pyoderma Facial), this illness with the physical and psychological impacts, turned her life upside down, with a loss of her self confidence for 18 Months.

During these 18 Months, she was cared for under a Specialist Dermatologist Consultant (Dr Layton) and Laser Re-surfacing; Dermatologist Consultant (Dr Seukeran), who worked together to steer her through this illness, giving her reassurance and confidence at all times, they would get her through this illness and overcome some of the scarring caused by this severe acne.
