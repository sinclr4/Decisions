KEITH RIGG

Consultant Transplant Surgeon

Keith Rigg is a Consultant Transplant Surgeon at Nottingham University Hospitals NHS Trust and has been involved in adult and paediatric transplantation for 25 years. He has a particularly interest in the ethical, legal and policy areas surrounding organ donation and transplantation.

He is the immediate past President of the British Transplantation Society, is a founding non-executive member of the Human Tissue Authority, chairs the campaigning group Transplant 2013 and chairs the Kidney Transplant Clinical Reference Group. He is the author of the transplant and access surgery patient information documents for EIDO Healthcare.
