KEVIN HOLTON

Head of Respiratory, Diabetes, Liver and Kidney Programmes DoH

Kevin Holton is Head of Respiratory, Diabetes, Liver and Kidney programmes at the Department of Health. Prior to his current position, Kevin worked at the Healthcare Commission, having previously been Private Secretary to three different Ministers of Health. Kevin has also worked in the NHS Executive London and South East. He has a Diploma in Health Studies and has worked for the Department of Health for over ten years.
