KIM MOONLIGHT

Regional antenatal screening coordinator

With a nursing, midwifery and public health background, Kim has over 30 years of experience in the NHS. She has worked across a range of sectors from primary care to tertiary services, including at strategic level regionally and nationally.

Since 2004 Kim has held the post of North East Regional Antenatal and Newborn Screening Coordinator. During this time, she has supported the UK National Screening Committee Antenatal and Newborn Screening programmes, leading on many regional service implementations, modernisations and sector wide service improvements.
