LAURA GUEST

Chief Executive- British Society for Rheumatology

Laura Guest is Chief Executive Officer of the British Society for Rheumatology, previously a Deputy Chief Executive and Director of Commissioning in a PCT.

She has an MSc in health care commissioning and has worked at Executive level across organisations from NHS Trusts to the Department of Health and the commercial sector, within London and nationally. Laura regularly presents at national conferences on leading edge developments such as clinical leadership and integrated care and delivering quality and productivity improvements. Publications include “Modernising the Accreditation and Regulatory Framework” 2006 and “Healthcare without Walls 2011”
