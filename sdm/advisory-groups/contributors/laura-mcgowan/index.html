LAURA MCGOWAN

Executive Director, Weight Concern

Dr Laura McGowan is a Chartered Psychologist with significant experience is the field of obesity prevention and behaviour change relating to weight management. Laura is employed by University College London and currently seconded to the national charity Weight Concern (Registered Charity No. 1059686) which aims to provide clear, evidence-based information on obesity and weight management. Laura has delivered numerous health-improving interventions and is responsible for directing Weight Concern’s research and dissemination strategies on obesity related issues. Laura has significant expertise in public health having also worked in a health promotion environment in Northern Ireland.
