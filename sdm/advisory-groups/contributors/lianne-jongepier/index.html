LIANNE JONGEPIER

EoE Clinical Respiratory Lead & Respiratory Services Manager

Lianne is a respiratory physiotherapist and currently works as the Respiratory Service Manager for ACE within NHS North East Essex, managing 16-staff in the COPD Service, Home Oxygen Assessment Service and the Pulmonary Rehabilitation Service. Additionally Lianne is East of England’s regional respiratory lead with Professor Tony Davison. This role involves supporting the region in achieving the Outcomes Framework for COPD & Asthma and its supporting documents, aiming to improve care for people with COPD & Asthma.

Lianne’s special interests are COPD Self-Management Education and Home Oxygen.
