LOUISE BRYANT

Lecturer in the Psychology of Healthcare

Dr Louise Bryant is a Chartered Psychologist researching in the area of patient information and informed choice in antenatal testing. She has worked with the NHS Fetal Anomaly Screening Programme and was an expert member of the working group who developed the UK Consent Standards for fetal anomaly and Down’s syndrome screening (2011). Louise lectures on social aspects of health and illness to medical students at the University of Leeds and the psychology of prenatal testing to Psychology undergraduates. She has a PhD in Understandings of Down’s syndrome in the Prenatal Testing Context.
