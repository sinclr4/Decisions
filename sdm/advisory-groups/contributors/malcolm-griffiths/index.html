MALCOLM GRIFFITHS

Consultant Obstetrician, Chair (@ NICE) of: Caesarean Section GDG; Quality Standards Topic Expert Group; Evidence Update Advisory Group. Vice-chair RCOG Guidelines Committee

Malcolm GRIFFITHS is an experienced obstetrician & gynaecologist who has held a consultant appointment since 1994. He has held and continues to hold responsible posts within NICE and RCOG. He has published a modest number of papers in scientific journals (25 papers in peer-reviewed journals, one book chapter and one book) and is regularly invited to give lectures. In 2010 he was awarded two accolades The David Liu Prize from the RCOG for the year’s best obstetric paper in the British Journal of Obstetrics & Gynaecology and the Soraya Dhillon Equality & Diversity Award for the care of pregnant women with HIV infection.

He has particular clinical interests and expertise in: Caesarean Section and vaginal birth after CS, HIV and Hepatitis, stillbirth, symphysis pubis dysfunction in pregnancy, and Road traffic accidents in pregnancy.

Current Appointments: Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology (1994-), Associate Medical Director (2012-), Regional College Advisor, RCOG East of England (2009-2012, reappointed 2012-), Chair NICE Quality Standard Topic Expert Group for Caesarean Section (2012-2013), Chair NICE Evidence Update for CG132 (Caesarean Section) (2012-2013), Vice-chair, RCOG, Clinical Guidelines Committee (2011-2014), Chair East of England Regional Development Group for O&G (2006-), Previous Appointments, Clinical Director Obstetrics & Gynaecology (2001-2007), Regional Chair CEMACH (2008-2011), and Chair NICE Guideline Development Group for Caesarean Section (2010-2012).
