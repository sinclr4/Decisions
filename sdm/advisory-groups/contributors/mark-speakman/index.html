MARK SPEAKMAN

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Consultant Urological Surgeon

Mark J Speakman was appointed Consultant Urologist in Taunton in early 1990 and has developed a busy practice in the management of benign and malignant prostate disease, incontinence and andrology.

He has undertaken research and published over 100 papers on smooth muscle physiology, the treatment of prostate and bladder diseases, alternatives to prostatectomy, into quality of life assessment and evidence based medicine in urology.

He trained in London, Oxford and Glasgow and completed an MRC research fellowship with Professor Alison Brading and Mr Joe Smith in Oxford.

Mark has been a member of the Council of the British Association of Urological Surgeons (BAUS) on two occasions and was Honorary Treasurer of the Association (2003-2005). He was elected Vice-President of BAUS (2012-2014) and will become President of BAUS (2014-2016).

In Taunton he was clinical director for surgery (1995-2000) and was appointed as director of research and development (1998-2004). He was Associate Medical Director to the Trust from 2004 - 2009.

He is on the editorial committee of the British Journal of Urology and has been a member of the editorial board of the European Journal of Urology. He is a specialist advisor to the U.K. National Institute for Clinical Excellence and a member of the Research Scholarship Review Panel of the Urology Foundation.
