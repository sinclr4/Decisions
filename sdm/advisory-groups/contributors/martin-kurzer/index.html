MARTIN KURZER

President of British Hernia Society/ Surgeon

Martin Kurzer specializes in hernia surgery, and is senior surgeon at the British Hernia Centre where he has worked since 1991.

He was one of the first surgeons in the UK to advocate the use of mesh for all abdominal hernias. His main interests are in local anaesthetic day-case surgery, the repair of complex recurrent groin hernias and groin pain in athletes.

He has one of the largest series of open local anaesthetic hernia repairs in Europe and has trained many surgeons from around the world in the open tension-free mesh technique. In 2007 he visited Ghana with the charity ‘Operation Hernia’.

He has published many peer reviewed articles and book chapters on all aspects of hernia surgery. www.martinkurzer.com

He is a co-founder of the British Hernia Society, of which he is currently president and is a member of the American and European Hernia Societies.
