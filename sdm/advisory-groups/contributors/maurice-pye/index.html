MAURICE PYE

Consultant Cardiologist

I am employed by the York Healthcare Trust.  I have been a Consultant Cardiologist since 1994.  I work at York Teaching Hospital and Leeds General Infirmary.

I am a general and interventional cardiologist. I am the Lead Clinician and senior cardiologist  on a busy cardiology unit caring for acutely ill patients with heart attacks, valvular heart disease, congenital heart disease and heart rhythm disorders.

I am a member of the British Cardiovascular Society and British Cardiovascular Interventional Society and Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians.

I have been head of Cardiology Research in York for past 15 years – I have published and continue to publish in major peer review journals.
