MICHAEL KELLY

Emeritus Consultant Colorectal Surgeon & National Advisor for Colorectal Cancer

Consultant Colorectal Surgeon, Leicester (1985-2009)

National Advisor for Colorectal Cancer

Cambridge & St George’s Hospital, London (scholar).

MB BChir ’70; MRCP(UK) 73; FRCS(Eng) ’74; MChir ‘78

Registrar & research senior registrar, Cambridge ’74-78; senior registrar rotation Bristol & Exeter ’79–‘85; RSO St Mark’s 1981.

President Coloproctology Section RSM 2011-2012; President St Mark’s Association 2010-2011; Chairman of the Clinical Governance Board, Association of Coloproctology GBI; Court of Examiners RCS (previous chairman of colorectal MCQ’s); Life Fellow & Trustee of HCSA. Editor Intestinal Section of the journal Surgery.

With colleagues at Leicester, as National Lead Clinician for Colorectal Cancer he has pioneered the Straight-to-Test system and MDT inauguration and development.
