MIKE HARRIS

Communications Lead, NHS Adult (non-cancer) Screening Programmes

Mike is responsible for the internal and external communications of the two NHS (non-cancer) adult screening programmes – the NHS AAA Screening Programme and the NHS Diabetic Eye Screening Programme. He has worked in NHS communications roles for nearly four years. A former journalist, he previously worked for nearly 20 years in a variety of reporting and editing roles for local and regional newspapers.
