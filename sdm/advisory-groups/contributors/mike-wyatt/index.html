MIKE WYATT

Consultant Vascular Surgeon

Mike Wyatt is a Consultant Vascular Surgeon and Honorary Reader at the Freeman Hospital, Newcastle upon Tyne. His major interest is in aortic aneurysm surgery and he runs a busy tertiary referral practice specializing in both open and endovascular techniques.

Mike is the Honorary Secretary of the Vascular Society of Great Britain and Ireland, Treasurer of the British Journal of Surgery Society, Clinical Editor of SURGERY and a senior iMRCS Examiner/Assessor on the RCSE Court of Examiners. He chairs the Northern Deanery Surgical Training Committee and is a past member of the General Surgery SAC.

He has edited three vascular textbooks and is a Senior Editor for the new Oxford Textbook of Surgery: Fundamentals of Surgery. We writes and lectures extensively in Vascular Surgery, and has served as President of the North of England Surgery Society, Editorial Secretary for ASGBI and Secretary/Editorial Secretary for SARS.
