MYRA STERN

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Consultant in Respiratory Medicine & Integrated Respiratory Care

Dr Myra Stern (MBChB, PhD, FRCP) is a Consultant in Respiratory Medicine and Integrated Respiratory Care – Whittington Health and NHS Islington, London UK. For the past 10 years she has been Clinical lead for Quit Smoking at the Whittington Hospital which now has a comprehensive quit smoking service for inpatients, outpatients and staff, as well as training for all frontline healthcare professionals. In 2011, she became chair of the British Thoracic Society Quit Smoking Champions, a newly established network of clinicians aimed at establishing a clinically led, comprehensive quit smoking service in every hospital in the country.
