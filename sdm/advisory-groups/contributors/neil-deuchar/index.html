NEIL DEUCHAR

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Associate Medical Director - Mental Health

Neil’s clinical portfolio has included General Community Psychiatry, Perinatal Mental Health, Eating disorders, Forensic Psychiatry and Neuropsychiatry and currently focuses on the Mental Health of homeless persons.

He has undertaken Medical Directorships in both specialist Mental Health and Strategic Health Authority settings, his managerial portfolio having included Leadership for Quality, Professional Regulation, clinical configurations, Information Governance and Research and Innovation.

Neil’s career has also taken him into general practice, journalism and media relations.

Clinical interests to include Holism in Psychiatric practice, medically unexplained symptoms, co-morbidity and complexity, and Psychiatry in Primary Care.

Neil’s managerial interests include Revalidation, the relationship of team function and culture to safety and effectiveness, Collaborative Commissioning, and Public Mental Health.

In addition, Neil also has roles as Consultant Psychiatrist to Birmingham Samaritans and Birmingham Diocese. He is also Director of Strategy for the Centre for Sustainability in Healthcare, Oxford, and a trustee of the Centre for Health and Healing, St Martin’s Church in the Bullring, Birmingham.

Neil has recently started a social enterprise that aims to provide personal development and healthcare employment opportunities for destitute people.
