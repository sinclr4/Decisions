NICKY MASON

Associate and Midwife Consultant

Nicky has been a midwife for 22 years and has practised in all clinical areas. In 2007 she joined the NHS Institute for Innovation and Improvement as a Midwife Consultant where she developed a national maternity service improvement tool. Since then she has provided direct support to maternity services across England and Wales through quality service improvement programmes.

Nicky is passionate about involving service users in research and health care design. She will shortly complete a PhD which is exploring women’s experience of planned caesarean birth in their first pregnancy.
