NIGEL CAPPS

Consultant Chemical Pathologist

Following degrees in Biochemistry and Medicine at Glasgow University and training around Glasgow and in Exeter, Dr Capps moved to Shropshire in 1989 to become Consultant Chemical Pathologist - covering the Princess Royal Hospital, Telford and the Royal Shrewsbury Hospitals, now The Shrewsbury & Telford Hospital NHS Trust.

He has had a major interest in lipids and atherosclerosis prevention for many years "from the test-tube to public health". He runs lipid and atherosclerosis prevention clinics at Shrewsbury and Telford and directs the local cardiovascular research group. He has a special interest in treating patients of all ages with Familial Hypercholesterolaemia and is chair of the medical, scientific and research committee of HEART UK.

He is Director of Research & Development at The Shrewsbury & Telford Hospital NHS Trust and Joint Clinical Director of West Midlands North Comprehensive Local Research Network.
