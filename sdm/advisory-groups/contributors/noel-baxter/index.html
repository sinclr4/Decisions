NOEL BAXTER

General Practitioner

Noel is a GP in Southeast London and is a local respiratory champion for NHS Southwark. Noel currently co-leads the NHS London respiratory team. He is an active member of the Primary Care Respiratory Society and represents PCRS-UK in IMPRESS. He works with the BLF as a member of the London and Southeast committee.
