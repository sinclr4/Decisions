PAUL HEWITSON

Senior Research Officer, Health Services Research Unit, University of Oxford

Paul has been involved in the development and evaluation of public information materials for the NHS Cancer Screening Programmes since 2002.  He has been involved in the publication of systematic reviews concerning prostate and bowel cancer screening, the European guidelines for communicating information in bowel cancer screening programmes, an evaluation of the information materials Prostate Cancer Risk Management Programme and the development of population and primary care information materials for the NHS Bowel Cancer Screening Programme.  Paul's main research interests include informed decision-making in population screening, implementation of evidence-based research and public perceptions of health interventions.
