PAUL MCARDLE

Co-Chair, Diabetes Management & Education Group of The British Dietetic Association. Lead Clinical Dietitian & Deputy Head of Dietetics: Birmingham Community Healthcare NHS Trust

Paul has been working as a Dietitian for over 11 years and has spent most of that time both in a community setting and specialising in diabetes. He is passionate about both patient and professional education and believes wholeheartedly in patient-centred care. In a voluntary capacity he is currently Co-Chair of the British Dietetic Association’s Diabetes Management and Education Group (DMEG), the specialist group for dietitians working diabetes, as well as being a member of the Professional Practice Board of the BDA and a media spokesperson. Paul’s NHS role is Lead Clinical Dietitian and Deputy Head of Nutrition in a busy community department in Birmingham. In 2012-13 Paul is undertaking a Masters in Research Methods at the University of Nottingham, under an NIHR Studentship, where his main research interest is carbohydrate in type 2 diabetes.
