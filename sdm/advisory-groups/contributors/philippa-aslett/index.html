PHILIPPA ASLETT

Senior Urology Nurse Specialist, CNS & NMP Lead

Having trained in 1886 in Chelmsford. Philippa moved to the field of Urology 1996 as a Urology Nurse Practitioner at Addenbrooke’s Hospital inCambridge. Here she helped to set up the Urology nursing service. Setting up and running nurse led clinics and developing innovative new services, such as Erectile dysfunction, Elevated PSA and LUTS clinics. Developing the intravesical chemotherapy and immunotherapy service for bladder cancer. In 2000 she set up and ran the Urology Course at Homerton College Cambridge and became a Lecturer practitioner

In 2003 she moved toBasingstoke. Here her role has developed and she now leads the Urology Nursing team inBasingstoke. Clinically her practice has become specialised to men with Prostate disease seeing new and follow up patients at all stages of their journey. She performs Transrectal ultrasound guided biopsies of the prostate. She has an active role teaching and lecturing locally and nationally.

Philippa became President of the British Association of Urology Nurses (BAUN) in November 2012.
