RACHAEL ROBINSON

GP and GPSI Dermatology

Dr Rachael Robinson has been a GP in Knaresborough,North Yorkshiresince 1997. She developed an interest in Dermatology and achieved her Post Graduate Diploma in Dermatology in 2003 fromCardiffUniversity. She now spends half the week working as a GP and a GP trainer and the other half working as a GP with a Special Interest in Dermatology atHarrogateHospitaland in a community clinic inNorth Manchester. She also sits on a Guideline Development Group for Melanoma Skin Cancer for the National Institute for Health and Clinical Excellence (NICE). Her clinical interests lie in Acne management and the psychological effects of having acne on patients.
