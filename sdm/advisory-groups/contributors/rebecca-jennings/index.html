REBECCA JENNINGS

Specialist Palliative Care Physiotherapist

Rebecca Jenningsis a specialist physiotherapist in palliative care. She has worked in New Zealand and the UK in diverse rehabilitation settings, before pursuing a career in palliative care at St Christopher’s Hospice and St Joseph’s Hospice in London, where she presently leads the Therapies team.

Rebecca is passionate about developing and promoting the role of physiotherapy within the field of palliative care and has a special interest in rehabilitation and enablement with a focus on health promotion. She has a Masters in Palliative Care (Kings College University) and has published on palliative rehabilitation, breathlessness and the interface between pain and function in advanced cancer. Rebecca contributes as a guest lecturer to under and postgraduate programmes at King’s College, University of East London and City University in London and is the Allied Health Professional national representative on the Help the Hospices Clinical Committee.
