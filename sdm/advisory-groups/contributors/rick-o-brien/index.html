RICK O&RSQUO;BRIEN

Social Care Lead, National End of Life Care Programme

Rick began his career in 1983, first working in community and voluntary sector projects in Liverpool. After qualifying as a social worker in 1986 he has worked in local government as a social work practitioner and training officer before taking up senior commissioning and operational social care management posts in South Yorkshire, Lancashire and more recently the Wirral. He has been the ADASS lead for end of life care since 2010 and has worked closely with the National End of Life Care Programme to implement the ‘living well, dying well social care framework’. He has recently started a secondment at the NEoLCP. He has a particular interest in improving standards of evidence based practice, integrated service models and building effective partnerships across health, housing and social care commissioners and service providers.
