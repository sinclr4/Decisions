ROBERT HENDERSON

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Consultant Cardiologist

Rob Henderson is consultant cardiologist at the Trent Cardiac Centre, Nottingham University Hospitals. His main interests are the interventional management of coronary and structural heart disease. He was a member of the NICE Acute Coronary Syndrome guideline development group and clinical advisor to the Stable Angina guideline development group. He is currently deputy chair of the NICE STEMI guideline development group and a specialist advisor to the NICE Interventional Procedures Programme. He is currently the clinical standards lead for the British Cardiovascular Intervention Society.
