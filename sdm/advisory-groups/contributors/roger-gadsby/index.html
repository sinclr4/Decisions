ROGER GADSBY

GP

Roger Gadsby is an Associate Clinical Professor at Warwick Medical School, University of Warwick and Visiting Professor at the Institute of Diabetes in Older People, the University of Bedfordshire. He was a GP for 31 years and has written 4 diabetes textbooks, 12 textbook chapters and over 300 papers and articles mainly on diabetes topics. At Warwick he has developed a number of assessed accredited courses in diabetes care. He is an expert advisor for the diabetes QoF at NICE and has been on the guideline development groups for 4 NICE diabetes guidelines. He was awarded an MBE in the Queen’s Birthday Honours list in 2009 for “services to medicine and diabetes care”. He has recently been appointed as GP Clinical Lead for the National Diabetes Audit, and as Primary Care lead for nhs diabetes.
