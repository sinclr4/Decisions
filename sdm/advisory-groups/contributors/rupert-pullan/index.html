RUPERT PULLAN

Consultant Colorectal Surgeon

Rupert Pullan is a consultant colorectal surgeon in Torquay, Devon and formerly Hunterian Professor of the Royal College of Surgeons of England. His professional interests include diagnosis and management of colorectal cancer, bowel cancer screening and advanced colonoscopy. He has led the Peninsular Network Bowel Cancer Site Specific Group and Bowel Cancer Screening Groups, sits on the JAG committee for GI endoscopy on behalf of the Association of Coloproctology and is Training Lead in Torbay Regional Endoscopy Training Unit. He is centre director for South Devon NHS Bowel Cancer Screening Centre.
