SARAH CANT

Interim Director of Policy & Campaigns, Prostate Cancer UK

Sarah joined Prostate Cancer UK in November 2009, and currently leads the organisation's Policy & Campaigns department. Her teams’ focus is to ensure that the charity has the evidence to understand men’s experiences of prostate cancer diagnosis and care, and to determine what good quality care and support looks like. This information is used to influence and improve national prostate cancer care and to monitor the charity’s impact.

Prior to this role, Sarah obtained a PhD in Pharmacology from University College London before working as an MRC Fellow at the Parliamentary Office of Science and Technology and a Policy Manager at Breakthrough Breast Cancer.

Prostate Cancer UK (formerly The Prostate Cancer Charity) isthe leading UK charity for men with prostate cancer and prostate problems. They fight to help more men survive prostate cancer and enjoy a better quality of life with a focus on supporting menand providing information; finding answers by funding research and leading change to raise awareness and improve care.
