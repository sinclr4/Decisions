SEAN KEHOE

Medical Advisory Group Lead: Consultant Gynaecologist

Sean Kehoe is a gynaecological cancer specialist. He is a Senior Research Fellow in St.Peters College Oxford and is presently the President of the British Gynaecological Cancer Society. He founded the Oxford Gynaecological Cancer Centre whilst working there and developed the regional service there for women with gynaecological cancers in the Thames Valley. He has been on Department of Health and other National Groups,is the Senior Medical Advisor for OVACOME a charity for ovarian cancer . He has undertaken research in gynaecological cancers and pre-cancer, and also led the CHORUS MRC trial – the largest even undertaken in the UK addressing the timing of surgery in advanced ovarian cancer. He is also a spokesperson for the RCOG, and presently an elected Fellow of Council. He has published over 120 paper/book chapters, edited 7 books on various topic in gynaecological and gynaecological cancers, and is regularly invited to lecture at National and Internal Meetings.
