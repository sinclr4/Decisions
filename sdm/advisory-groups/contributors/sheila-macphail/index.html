SHEILA MACPHAIL

Consultant Obstetrician

Sheila Macphail qualified in Southampton and has worked in the North East of England since 1984. She spent 2 years in Toronto before being appointed to a consultant post in Fetal Medicine and Obstetrics in Newcastle in 1995. She has been actively involved in both undergraduate and postgraduate education and is currently the Director of Medical Education in the Newcastle Hospitals NHS Trust. She has been Clinical Director for Women’s Services and is currently an Assistant Medical Director with responsibility for education in the Trust. Sheila has been involved in the Newcastle end of the MAGIC Shared Decision Making project project from the beginning and has worked as a clinical lead to implement SDM within maternity care as well as helping to ensure wider implementation within the trust.
