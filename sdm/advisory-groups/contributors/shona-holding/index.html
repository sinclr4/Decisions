SHONA HOLDING

Arrhythmia Nurse Specialist

Shona trained inLeedsand her nursing experience has predominately been within cardiology and cardio-thoracic surgery. Junior and senior posts have been based at The National Heart,ChelseaandWestminsterandRoyalBromptonHospitalsinLondonas well as Leeds General Infirmary. She was ward manager of a cardiac surgical/HDU ward at the Yorkshire Heart Centre for a number of years and then joined the West Yorkshire Cardiac Network as a Service Improvement Manager for 2 years. She was then appointed the Arrhythmia Nurse Specialist post in 2007.

Over the past 6 years, Shona’s role as an arrhythmia nurse has evolved with the support of BHF and she is now employed by Leeds Community Healthcare Trust. She works closely with two hospital based arrhythmia nurses and together they provide an arrhythmia service across primary, secondary and tertiary care. She has a special interest in management of Atrial Fibrillation (AF) and has been involved in the implementation and successful running of a Rapid Access Nurse Led Atrial Fibrillation clinic. She was also involved in the development of the GRASP-AF tool, which is being rolled out acrossEngland. (A primary care IT tool that identifies patients with an existing diagnosis of AF who are not on appropriate treatment for stroke prevention).

She has been author and co-author of a number of publications in nursing journals and presents regularly at regional and national events on AF related topics.
