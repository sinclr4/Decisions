SIMON P KELLY

Consultant Ophthalmic Surgeon, Bolton Hospital NHS Foundation Trust

Mr Kelly’s clinical interests are in medical retina and cataract surgery. His professional interests include quality and safety. He has served as a member of the Council and of Professional Standards Committee of the Royal College of Ophthalmologists and as chair of the College's Quality and Safety Sub-Committee. In 2011 he was a contributer  to cataract patient decision aid (PDA) produced by the College with NHS Direct.  He has also assisted the Foundation for Informed Medical Decision Making with producing their cataract PDA.
