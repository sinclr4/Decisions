SION GLYN-JONES

Senior Lecturer in Musculoskeletal Sciences and Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon

Mr Sion Glyn-Jones read medicine at Cambridge and later conducted his clinical attachment in London. Thereafter, he went on to complete his surgical and orthopaedic training in Oxford, where he gained a D.Phil. He is a Senior Lecturer at the University of Oxford, where he runs the Hip Research Unit. He also works as a Consultant at the Nuffield Orthopaedic Centre, where he runs the Young Adult Hip Service.
