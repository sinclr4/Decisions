SUE WOODWARD

Lay representative

Sue Woodward is chair of the Patient Liaison Group at the Royal college of Surgeons where she has been a member for the last 5 years. Sue works within the pharmaceutical industry in drug safety with a particular interest in patient support groups. Previously she has been involved in the development and co ordination of clinical trial across Europe. Both her professional and personnel experiences of the NHS have given her a keen interest in the patient perspective and particularly the patient pathway and accessibility to high quality appropriate services and research. She has been involved in developing various surgical patient journey aids and documentation for patients to guide them to correct information on their surgical experience.
