I have been a Principal in General Practice at Rowcroft Medical Centre in Stroud for 26 years. I have an interest in Men’s Health, particularly in the preventative aspects. We, as Health Professionals need to advocate shared decision-making and encourage the individual to take responsibility for his or her health by informing them of their choices and treatment options.
