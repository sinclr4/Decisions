VANESSA BEATTIE

Lung CNS

Having trained in Liverpool, Vanessa was based within the Thoracic Medicine Department Of Aintree Hospital. This experience gave Vanessa an interest in working with patients with Lung Cancer. Prior to becoming a Lung CNS Vanessa was a Deputy Ward Manager for the Medical Admissions Unit. She has been a Lung CNS at Aintree Hospital for 12 years. Her interests include streamlining the patient pathway; Holistic Needs assessment; improving patient & carer experience across the cancer pathway. Vanessa is a committee member of the National Lung Cancer Forum for Nurses (NLCFN) and Chair for the Thoracic Surgical Group (a sub group of the NLCFN).
