VICTORIA TODD

Chief Executive

Victoria has been Chief Executive of Cancerkin since November 2008. Her background is mainly in the arts where posts she has held include Director of the National Campaign for the Arts, Director of the Council for Dance Education and Training, Chief Executive of the United Kingdom Institute for Conservation of Historic Works of Art and positions in the Tate Gallery and UNESCO. She was also Director of a small development charity working mainly on delivering support to children with AIDS in Kenya. A consistent theme running through her career and voluntary work has been concern for the deprived or marginalized. To contact Victoria phone her on 02078302323 or email her on vtodd@cancerkin.org.uk
