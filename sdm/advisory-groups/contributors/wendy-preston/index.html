WENDY PRESTON

Advanced Nurse Practitioner and Senior Lecturer

Qualified as an RGN at Coventry School of Nursing in 1992 and then worked in general medicine and respiratory. Bachelor of Science (Hons) studies 2005-2007 structured the development of a Respiratory Support Service whilst being a respiratory ward manager to provide pro-active acute outpatient care. Recently Wendy has completed a Master of Science degree in respiratory care and advanced practitioner qualifications. The respiratory support service has set up an innovative secondary care stop smoking service targeting patients, visitors, staff and relatives. It also facilitates clinics for hard to reach populations e.g. teenagers and routine and manual workers.

Wendy currently works at George Eliot Hospital and has a portfolio of roles which includes; ambulatory care lead (which incorporates the respiratory support service), advanced nurse practitioner, stop smoking service manager and BTS stop smoking champion. She is seconded to Coventry University two days per week as a senior lecturer in prescribing and adult nursing and is developing a degree online module in smoke cessation.

Nationally, Wendy is a committee member of the association of respiratory nurses (ARNS), teaching on their national end of life course (incorporating Sage&Thyme communication training). Also on the steering committee for the BTS stop smoking champions.
