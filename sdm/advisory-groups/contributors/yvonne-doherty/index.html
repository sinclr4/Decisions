YVONNE DOHERTY

Consultant Clinical Psychologist

Yvonne Doherty qualified as a Clinical Psychologist in 1993 and completed her Clinical Doctorate in Illness Representations and Diabetes Self Management in 2001. She maintained an interest Physical Health; its impact on Psychological Well Being and the influence of Psychological Processes on Self Management and Self Care. Clinically, she has worked in Diabetes, Burns and Plastics, HIV and AIDS, Cardiology, Weight Management, Oncology and Palliative Care and as a Primary Care Psychologist. She began working in Diabetes in 1995 on a Diabetes UK funded project which sought to train health care professionals in what was then termed 'Behaviour Change Counselling'. She was on the first SIGN guideline group that reviewed the evidence for diabetes self management in 2001, and more latterly the NICE Diabetes Quality Standards. Her particular interests have been in the provision of effective partnership working in both Structured Education and Clinical Consultations. She was part of the core team that developed DESMOND intervention and training package 10 years ago and was involved in the National Year of Care pilots and the development of the National Training Package. Yvonne is currently employed by both Leicester Diabetes Centre as a Senior Research Associate and The Year of Care Partnerships.
